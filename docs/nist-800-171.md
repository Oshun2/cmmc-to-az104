# NIST SP 800-171 — The Standard Behind CMMC Level 2

**CMMC Level 2 is not a separate control set — it *is* NIST SP 800-171.** Every Level 2 practice in this repository is one of the 110 security requirements defined in **NIST Special Publication 800-171**. This document summarizes the standard so the mappings make sense in context.

> **Sources:** [NIST SP 800-171 (csrc.nist.gov)](https://csrc.nist.gov/pubs/sp/800/171/r2/upd1/final), [32 CFR Part 170 Final Rule](https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2024/10/15/2024-22905/cybersecurity-maturity-model-certification-cmmc-program). Verify the applicable revision against your contract's DFARS clauses.

---

## What It Is

**NIST SP 800-171 — *Protecting Controlled Unclassified Information in Nonfederal Systems and Organizations*** — defines the security requirements a **non-federal organization** (e.g., a defense contractor) must meet to protect **CUI** when it lives on the contractor's own systems rather than federal systems.

- Published by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST).
- Required for DoD contractors via **DFARS 252.204-7012** since 2017, and now the basis of **CMMC Level 2**.
- **CMMC currently anchors to NIST SP 800-171 Revision 2** (see "Revisions" below).

## Companion Documents

| Publication | Role |
|-------------|------|
| **NIST SP 800-171** | The 110 security **requirements** (the "what") |
| **NIST SP 800-171A** | The **assessment objectives and procedures** (the "how to assess") — each requirement breaks into lettered objectives like `3.1.1[a]`, `[b]`, `[c]` that must *all* be met |
| **NIST SP 800-172** | **Enhanced** requirements for high-value assets — the basis of **CMMC Level 3** |
| **NIST SP 800-53** | The broader federal control catalog that 800-171 is derived from (used by FedRAMP/federal systems) |

This repo's evidence guidance ([demonstrating-compliance.md](demonstrating-compliance.md)) is framed around the **800-171A** assessment methods — **Examine, Interview, Test**.

## Revisions: R2 vs R3

| Revision | Published | Status for CMMC |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Revision 2** | Feb 2020 | **The version CMMC uses today.** The 32 CFR 170 rule and DoD Assessment Methodology are built on R2's 110 requirements. |
| **Revision 3** | May 2024 | Newer (restructured families, revised requirement count, added Organization-Defined Parameters). **Not yet the CMMC baseline** — CMMC continues to reference R2 until DoD formally transitions. |

> **Practical takeaway:** Build to **Revision 2 (110 requirements)** for CMMC Level 2 today, but track R3 for future transition. This repository maps to **R2**.
